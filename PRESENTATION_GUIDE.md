"Good morning. Today I'll present our comprehensive analysis of 2019 sales data, covering 185,000 orders and $34.5 million in revenue. We've identified four key opportunities that could increase revenue by 45% with minimal investment."

### Geographic Section (2 minutes)
"Our data shows a clear geographic concentration. California accounts for 38% of all orders, with San Francisco and Los Angeles leading. This presents both an opportunity and a risk. We recommend doubling down on California while diversifying into similar markets."

### Timing Section (2 minutes)
"Customer behavior shows distinct patterns. 70% of orders occur between 9 AM and 9 PM, with December and October as peak months. By aligning our advertising with these patterns, we can improve conversion rates by an estimated 20%."

### Product Section (2 minutes)
"Accessories drive volume, but our analysis reveals something more interesting: customers frequently buy specific product combinations. 35% of phone buyers also purchase charging cables. This creates a perfect opportunity for bundling."

### Recommendations (3 minutes)
"Based on these insights, we propose four initiatives:

1. **Product Bundles:** Create three packages based on common combinations. Low investment, high return.

2. **Ad Optimization:** Shift 80% of ad spend to 9 AM - 9 PM window. Immediate implementation.

3. **Inventory Management:** Increase stock for top products and pre-stock before October. Prevents lost sales.

4. **Geographic Focus:** Strengthen California presence while testing expansion markets.

Together, these initiatives require $350K investment but project $15.6M additional revenue - a 4,357% ROI."

### Closing (1 minute)
